Bruce Project Management is led by Nigel Bruce Pasco, a Chartered Professional Civil Engineer with more than twenty years on major Australian infrastructure — including delivery and administration roles on the Pacific Motorway, Gold Coast Light Rail and Pacific Highway programs.

Nigel's career spans contractor-side construction delivery and principal-side superintendency and program management, with deep working knowledge of the Australian standard contracts (AS4000, AS4300, AS2124, GC21) and TMR technical specifications. That combination — builder's pragmatism, superintendent's rigour — is what clients engage Bruce to bring.
